Pacman Tetris WS

A wider, meaner sibling to the first mashup: steer Pac-Man through a live pellet field while tetrominoes keep dropping into the same board. Arrow keys move Pac-Man. A, D, W, S, and Space drive the active piece.

Row clears stun the ghosts. Power pellets flip the hunt. If a block lands on Pac-Man, the cabinet wins.

Serve locally with python3 -m http.server 4174 --directory examples/pacman-tetris-ws.
